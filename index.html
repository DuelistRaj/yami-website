Good Guys

The Yamites are the species of Yuyo and Yuki, and many of the sentient NPCs that they will come across in their journeys. The player must remember though, looks can be deceiving! An ally may be discovered when hope seems lost, yet a friendly face may be a mask for deceit.

Power-Ups

As the brothers grow in their journey, so too does their abilities and strength. After defeating powerful foes or aiding those in need, Yuyo and Yuki gain new spells and magic, able to rise to each new challenge with their newfound skills and powers.

Bad Guys

The creatures inhabiting Kinfallen Forest do not take kindly to strangers, and will not tolerate wanderers through their territories. Shrubbles and uprooters make this forest their home, and have become twisted from corruption to show aggression without provocation.

Malfelissa, the First Boss

Malfelissa, once was an exotic bloom of the Kinfallen forest. Corrupted by dark magic it now stands in the way of the brothers to the Forlorn Empire.

However deep the roots of Malfelissa may seem she possess an obvious weakness which the brothers must find if they want to save Yuyo, the younger brother and the kingdom.

The Wandering Nomad

Once an adventurous pioneer from the Final City, the wandering nomad now resides on the hills above the Kinfallen forest.

Our brothers Yuyo and Yuki discover an unlikely ally within the Kinfallen Forest: The Lingering Nomad. He provides insight and training for the challenges ahead, and is one of the few friends they find on their quest.

Tresswing, a Flying Creature

Tresswings rest within the canopies of the Kinfallen forest, swooping down when prey walks beneath them.

They will attack from above, then fly upwards, continuing to attack in a parabolic state until the threat has left their territory.
